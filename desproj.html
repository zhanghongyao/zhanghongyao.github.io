DES 315 - Technology Research

Fall 2021 [September 2021 - December 2021]

 
 
 
×    

About:
Building a furniture assembly program using virtual reality.

Team Size: 3

Overview:
Virtual Reality (VR) creates a Virtual Environment with scenes and objects that simulates the environment in real life.
As it displays a virtual environment, VR has a large usage of fields. It creates a immersive environment that makes the program innovating and enjoyable.
Our purpose is to make use of VR to simulate the instructions given on the manual so that consumers can get a better understanding of the build before actually attempting to build the furniture.
Throughout the project, we added in visual and audio feedbacks (ref middle pic) that can assist players in building as well as advance features such as hammering(ref third pic).

Software/Hardware used:
Oculus Rift
3DS Max
Unreal Engine
