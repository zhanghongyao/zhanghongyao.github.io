About:
Blazing Blizzard is a 2D platformer where you play as a fire creature named Ember. Ember is the "Overlord of all" from the fire realm. However, she ended up in the blizzard realm while searching for her friend Fpider. In this realm, any fire creatures will be weakened, making her lose control over her body. However, being an overlord, she has managed to retain her powers. This allows her to sense Fpider’s presence and later find out that he is trapped and split into two (body and soul) to survive the harsh weather conditions. Fpider needs Ember’s help and now Ember needs you.
